# Power Progression Direction — From Spark to Catastrophe

**Status:** Approved product direction on 2026-07-23. Documentation only. This
does not authorize catalogue expansion, progression implementation, new saves,
balance work, Ink, Goal 7C implementation, Goal 7D, or any production change.

## The question

The accepted slices prove that one body can Burn, build a Load Source, and
address an autonomous Agent. They do not yet answer the long RPG question:

> How does a person who can scorch one creature become someone who can kill a
> dragon, lead an army, or destroy a city?

The answer must create a power fantasy without reducing every outcome to
character level, one universal Magic Power statistic, or a mana bar. Power
should grow through several legible dimensions and leave consequences in the
Chronicle.

## Working progression model

Power grows through three connected layers.

| Layer | What grows | What it answers | Persistence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Body | physical capability: health, equipment, traits, training, and whichever body-advancement model is later approved | How capable is this Incarnation? | ends or changes with the body |
| Language | Codex breadth, active Verb slots, Link capacity, Load, and expressive Modifiers | What magic can the player currently express? | Codex and most capacity survive death |
| Leverage | relationships, recognized authority, allies, Holdings, Load Sources, artifacts, rare materials, and world position | What can this power reach and sustain? | material, social, vulnerable, and historical |

The recommended role for conventional levels is therefore bounded but real.
An Incarnation may become physically stronger, tougher, more skilled, and more
capable of surviving Preparation. Level must not automatically multiply every
Verb's damage or make every Agent obedient. Words remain more valuable than
levels because they open new actions and new scales of consequence.

`Body` does not settle levels, attributes, skill points, or another advancement
model by implication. It names the body-scoped capability layer. Any specific
advancement model still requires its own proof against death, replacement, and
the durable value of the Codex.

## A Verb begins with an authored effect

Every Verb should own a readable baseline action plan. For an offensive Verb,
that includes real numbers such as damage, range, Preparation, consequence,
and Recovery. Utility, physical, minion, and wildcard Verbs need equally
concrete bases appropriate to their action.

`Burn` currently proves this with bounded authored damage and timing. Future
growth should transform the plan through reusable dimensions rather than add
`if BurnRank >= 12` branches.

Candidate dimensions include:

- **potency:** damage, healing, force, or strength of change;
- **scale:** size of one Target or number/area of Targets;
- **reach:** physical or social distance;
- **duration:** how long a consequence or permission lasts;
- **precision:** how narrowly the effect discriminates;
- **penetration:** which resistance, armor, ward, or material thresholds it
  can overcome;
- **repetition:** whether the action repeats, echoes, chains, or propagates;
- **notice:** who or what becomes aware of the act;
- **stability:** misfire, interruption, mutation, and collateral risk; and
- **persistence:** whether the result ends with the action or enters Chronicle
  history as durable state.

Not every Verb uses every dimension. Compatibility is authored. Modifiers are
unique and order-independent within one Expression, retain fixed authored
Load, and should change play rather than provide invisible percentage filler.

## Power must change presentation

An action-plan dimension is not complete when only its arithmetic changes. Core
owns the exact facts; presentation must give those facts a spatial, textual,
motion, or sound consequence appropriate to the decision.

| Dimension | Required player-facing change |
| --- | --- |
| potency | the affected body or material responds with proportionate force, damage, healing, or transformation, with the decisive source available in the breakdown |
| scale | preview and resolution occupy the changed Target size, group, area, or geographic footprint |
| reach | the map shows the path, connection, line of effect, social delivery, or vertical span that became possible |
| duration | continuing activity and its remaining timing stay legible without a permanent generic cast bar |
| precision | selected and excluded subjects are distinguishable before commitment |
| penetration | the defeated or surviving Armor, ward, resistance, or material layer visibly answers the effect |
| repetition | echoes, chains, or propagation retain ordered origins, Targets, and timing |
| notice | witnesses, sound, signs, or later Pressure reflect who could perceive the act |
| stability | misfire, interruption, mutation, and collateral risk are forecast rather than revealed as arbitrary punishment |
| persistence | the result leaves a durable map state, subject condition, relationship, or Chronicle history where the rule promises one |

The exact visual and audio language remains slice-specific, but a Modifier may
not pass merely because a tooltip number changed. The approved
[Goal 7C contract](GOAL-7C-FROM-HARNESS-TO-GAME.md), supported by the
[Experience Pass 1 direction](EXPERIENCE-PASS-1-FROM-HARNESS-TO-GAME.md),
owns the first bounded proof of this presentation rule over already accepted
mechanics.

## Ten candidate Verbs

This is a breadth test for the eventual Word Catalogue, not the next production
batch. It deliberately covers different player fantasies and proves that the
grammar cannot be designed around Burn alone.

| Category | Verb | Base fantasy | Important distinction |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Offensive magic | **Burn** | apply heat, ignition, and continuing fire damage to a valid Target | fire resistance, fuel, matter, and collateral remain meaningful |
| Offensive magic | **Freeze** | remove heat, slow motion, make matter brittle, or immobilize | not blue Burn; temperature, brittleness, and containment create different consequences |
| Utility: Home/allies | **Build** | assemble or reshape matter into a selected structure or useful form | does not replace `Found`; Found establishes Home, while Build changes material within a place |
| Utility: Home/allies | **Mend** | restore a damaged creature, object, structure, bond, or bounded condition | cannot erase death, history, or every loss by default |
| Physical combat | **Smash** | deliver direct bodily or wielded force against a resisting Target | depends on body, stance, equipment, matter, and reach |
| Physical combat | **Guard** | intercept, brace, shelter, or hold space against a predicted threat | creates protection and positioning rather than passive Armor inflation |
| Minions/allies | **Call** | attract, summon, awaken, or request the presence of fitting Agents or entities | what answers depends on place, relationship, ecology, and the Expression |
| Minions/allies | **Command** | express dangerous or authority-gated intent to one or more Agents | makes an attempt admissible but never guarantees willing obedience |
| Wildcard | **Fly** | move a valid Target through air and between reachable vertical places | scale, duration, carrying, and atmosphere distinguish a hop from world travel |
| Wildcard | **Transform** | change a Target's bounded form, material, body plan, or state | identity, reversibility, precision, and persistence become central risks |

These are not ten mutually exclusive classes. A builder may Burn, a warrior
may Call, and a later Incarnation may reinterpret the same Codex completely.

## Ten candidate Modifiers

“Extension word” is useful plain language, but **Modifier** remains the
canonical domain term. The Path of Exile inspiration is support-like
transformation: linking a Modifier should change how a Verb plays, what it can
affect, or what cost it creates. Palimpsest does not copy gem sockets, colors,
levels, or currency systems.

| Modifier | Primary transformation | Typical price or tension |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Quickly** | shortens Preparation or accelerates the first resolution | high Load, instability, reduced precision, or greater notice |
| **Quietly** | reduces sound, signs, notice, or social visibility | lower potency, slower resolution, or narrower scale |
| **Vast** | increases affected size, area, group, or geographic scale | large Load, ritual time, material power, collateral, and notice |
| **Lasting** | extends duration or makes a consequence harder to reverse | Recovery, upkeep, persistence risk, or permanent collateral |
| **Far** | increases physical, vertical, or social reach | weaker precision, line-of-effect constraints, or greater Preparation |
| **Focused** | concentrates force and precision into fewer or smaller Targets | gives up area, chaining, or forgiving Target selection |
| **Piercing** | overcomes or passes through an authored resistance layer | high Load, narrower compatibility, reduced base potency, or collateral behind the Target |
| **Chaining** | propagates to additional valid Targets in sequence | declining potency, unpredictable path, or increased notice |
| **Echoing** | repeats the action or consequence after a delay | delayed commitment, repeated cost, or vulnerability to interruption |
| **Unstable** | trades reliability for exceptional potency or unusual outcomes | misfire, mutation, collateral, self-harm, or attracting Pressure |

No Modifier should be the universally correct damage support. A Modifier that
only increases a hidden scalar without changing a decision is suspect. Each
must have authored compatibility and a player-visible tradeoff.

Before a candidate Modifier enters a production contract, it must prove all of
the following:

- it changes at least one decision in the authored action plan rather than only
  multiplying an output;
- its changed behavior and price are visible or audible in preview and result;
- omitting it remains a defensible build choice; and
- a breadth fixture demonstrates useful behavior with at least two
  meaningfully different Verbs, unless an intentionally narrow compatibility
  is itself the authored identity being tested.

For example, `Quickly` changes exposure and transfers cost; `Far` changes the
connection and its constraints; `Focused` gives up footprint or forgiving
selection; and `Piercing` identifies a resistance layer and any consequence
behind it. None is accepted as a hidden percentage bonus.

## Expression stress tests

The grammar should be able to explain these ambitions without inventing a new
progression system for each one:

- **Kill a dragon in one release:** `Burn + Focused + Piercing` may concentrate
  sufficient heat through an authored weak layer, but a truly fire-immune
  dragon remains the wrong Target for Burn. Level alone cannot erase identity.
- **Burn a city:** `Burn + Vast + Lasting + Unstable` targets a district-scale
  place through a ritual, major Load, rare material power, obvious notice, and
  catastrophic collateral. The result becomes Chronicle history.
- **Raise a fortress quickly:** `Build + Vast + Quickly` demands matter,
  planning, infrastructure, and an exposed commitment instead of conjuring a
  free settlement from a button.
- **Protect a crowd:** `Guard + Vast + Lasting` trades personal mobility and
  offensive opportunity for sustained protection over a place or group.
- **Bring distant allies:** `Call + Far + Echoing` reaches fitting Agents over
  time; willingness, route, danger, and relationship still decide who arrives.
- **Lead an army:** `Command + Vast + Lasting` can express intent to a large
  recognized group, but every relevant Agent or command structure still has a
  reason to comply, reinterpret, delay, defect, or refuse.
- **Cross a World unseen:** `Fly + Far + Quietly` changes reach and notice, but
  still pays for carrying, atmosphere, duration, and destination constraints.
- **Become something impossible:** `Transform + Lasting + Unstable` offers
  enormous body fantasy at the cost of identity, reversibility, and mutation.

These examples are target-specific stories, not recipes guaranteed to exist
unchanged. A future proof that selects one must name the decisive Target,
material, position, relationship, and world facts; demonstrate at least one
materially different solution through Body, Language, or Leverage; and include
one superficially similar Target for which the named combination fails. A
dragon's exposed weak layer might yield to focused penetrating fire, while
another player first breaks the layer with `Smash`, freezes a heat-dependent
organ, or changes the battlefield. A truly fire-immune dragon remains the
wrong Target for Burn.

Targets expose facts and resistance; the interface must support discovery and
informed prediction without handing the player one mandatory Word combination.

## Why Command succeeds

Command's force answers only: **may this dangerous or authority-gated intent be
attempted?** Agent acceptance answers a different set of questions:

- Can this Agent understand and physically perform the Directive?
- Does the Agent recognize the issuer's role or authority?
- What relationship, oath, debt, loyalty, fear, or shared purpose exists?
- Does the Agent want the outcome?
- What danger, sacrifice, collateral, or conflicting commitment is expected?
- Which recent memories make trust or refusal reasonable?
- Is there a command structure that can carry intent across a large group?

These may be weighted or thresholded internally, but the result must retain
plain causal reasons rather than expose an unexplained persuasion score.

If the game eventually allows forced obedience, that is not a stronger
Command. It requires a distinct coercive capability such as binding,
possession, domination, oath magic, or a material control mechanism. Its
resistance, duration, rebellion, ethical cost, and historical consequences
must be explicit.

## Mana, Magic Power, and Ink

### Mana

Do not add a universal mana bar merely because the game contains magic.
Preparation, Recovery, Load, position, material requirements, notice, and
interruption already create costs. Ordinary combat and exploration should not
become a cycle of waiting for a blue bar.

### Magic Power

Do not let one global Magic Power statistic multiply every Verb. Bodies may
have traits that affect concentration, resilience, precision, or safe
amplification, but each Verb and Target retain authored meaning.

### Ink

**Ink is a strong candidate, not a settled term or resource.** Its best role is
rare material power used to exceed ordinary body-scale limits, stabilize a
persistent Expression, inscribe a transformation, or fuel a world-scale
ritual. It should not become mana with a thematic name.

Questions that must be answered before Ink becomes canonical:

- Is Ink carried matter, stored infrastructure, a mark on the Chronicle, or
  several authored substances?
- Is it consumed, committed, recoverable, or capable of staining a place?
- Can ordinary Invocations work without it?
- Does using it attract Agents, factions, creatures, or Palimpsests?
- What visible consequence distinguishes a vast Ink-powered act from a normal
  Expression?

## Growth cadence

The intended cadence is:

1. improve the current body enough to survive and act confidently;
2. discover Words that create genuinely new actions;
3. expand Load and Links to express deeper combinations;
4. earn relationships, materials, infrastructure, and positions that make
   larger consequences possible;
5. risk those accumulated advantages on acts the Chronicle will remember.

Repeatedly casting Burn must not grind Burn ranks. Use creates situations,
evidence, enemies, discoveries, and Study opportunities. Growth follows
consequence rather than button repetition.

## Design guardrails

- Power is the point of the game; balance should create ambitions and
  tradeoffs, not sand every ability down to symmetry.
- A one-shot is valid when the player assembled, discovered, or exploited the
  conditions that made it possible.
- More power should reveal larger Targets and consequences, not only larger
  floating numbers.
- Body progression must matter without making death erase the real game.
- Social growth must create reasons for cooperation without reducing Agents to
  units.
- World-scale power must be spectacular, interruptible where appropriate, and
  materially expensive enough to become history.
- Player-facing copy and visuals describe the fiction first. Exact numbers and
  breakdowns remain available for informed builds.

## Decisions still required

Before production, review and settle:

1. whether Incarnations use explicit levels, attribute advancement, skill
   advancement, or some combination;
2. which action-plan dimensions are universal values versus capability-specific
   facts;
3. whether potency ever derives partly from a body trait;
4. the first cross-Verb Modifier compatibility matrix;
5. whether `Transform` is an ordinary catalogue Verb or a rarer later horizon;
6. the line between Call, Command, taming, binding, and true domination;
7. whether Ink belongs in the game and, if so, what it physically is; and
8. which one future vertical slice can prove growth without implementing the
   whole progression model.

Until those decisions are approved, this document is direction and a design
test only.
