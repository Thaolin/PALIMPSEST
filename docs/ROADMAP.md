# Roadmap

Every milestone ends in one player-observable proof. A slice owns its language,
simulation, Lens, persistence, and tests together; there are no separate
“engine first” milestones. Development order does not imply that a capability
appears early in the finished game's progression.

## Horizon I — Knowledge shapes the Lens

### 0.2 — One apple means one apple

**Implemented vertical slice.**

Introduce the bounded typed PALI document and replace the normal raw-source
inspector with the mouse-first structured Lens. A normal Knowledge profile has
Entity Reach only: nourishment is changed through a typed State control and
becomes a sparse Local Override, leaving another apple unchanged without
freezing unrelated inherited meaning. Existing Behavior is rendered as
readable, non-patchable Clauses.

Prototype Reach is retained, validated, saved, and tested through a developer
Knowledge profile, but normal play neither grants nor advertises “every apple.”
Raw source moves behind explicit developer mode. See
[`INTERACTION.md`](INTERACTION.md) for the full acceptance contract.

### 0.3 — Knowledge changes what can be seen

Replace the fixed Lens projection with a small discoverable lexicon. Prove all
four Concept Access states: one attribute is Unperceived, one is Veiled, one is
Readable, and one is Patchable. The player learns a concept that changes the
Lens without changing the Universe or Embodiment; a qualitative value gains an
exact notation through a separate Revelation.

### 0.4 — Behavior has grammar

Make the existing `on use(actor)` Behavior patchable through typed Clauses.
Only known, type-compatible triggers, operands, and effects may be selected.
The proof is changing one apple's use Behavior entirely with the mouse, safely
rejecting an over-budget candidate, and preserving its Patch through save/load.

## Horizon II — Reach changes meaning

### 0.5 — Descendants remember

Add one deterministic tree-to-fruit reproduction loop and stable parentage.
Introduce Lineage Reach and Provenance. The proof is changing what one tree's
future apples inherit without retroactively rewriting already materialized
fruit or unrelated trees.

### 0.6 — Every apple

Make Prototype Reach discoverable in player progression. The Lens reveals a
broader target only after the relevant Revelation and previews both currently
materialized Entities and future Prototype inheritance. The proof is knowingly
binding one Patch to every apple while preserving narrower Entity and Lineage
Scars.

This is a late-game capability in the player's arc even though its underlying
mechanism and developer tests exist from the first slice.

### 0.7 — What counts as an apple

Introduce one Archetype as a semantic membership definition, distinct from the
Prototype that produced an Entity. The proof is making a non-apple qualify as
an apple, or an apple cease to qualify, and observing Behavior query that
meaning rather than a hard-coded Prototype enum.

### 0.8 — A single physical law

Route nourishment for apples and one other food through one typed Law. Expose a
budgeted, reversible Universe-Reach Patch with Anomaly reporting. The proof is
one Clause change affecting both foods compositionally, including an Entity
admitted through the Archetype from 0.7.

## Horizon III — The player is separable

### 0.9 — Body is not self

Add death for one Embodiment and one alternate inhabitable creature. The
Universe continues and Knowledge persists across the transfer. The proof is
losing a body without losing learned language or world Scars.

### 0.10 — History can be inspected

Record accepted Patches and major Scars in a compact append-only history. Give
them stable content identities before adding branches. The proof is inspecting
why the current apple differs from Genesis and reverting one historical Patch.

### 0.11 — One branch, deliberately

Create one explicit alternate ascended state from recorded history. The proof
is switching between two compact branches with shared Genesis and independently
preserved Scars. No offline historical simulation is included.

Larger terrain through deterministic Site Blueprints, broader ecology, more
Laws, and a general-purpose language wait until these proofs show that
Knowledge, Reach, meaning, identity, and history compose. See
[`GENERATION.md`](GENERATION.md) for the spatial generation contract those
later slices must obey.
