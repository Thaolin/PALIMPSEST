# Use one character scale across generated Areas

**Status:** accepted

The Chronicle uses one character-scale grid for open territory, Holdings,
dungeons, temples, ruins, and nested generated spaces. Distinct Areas connect
through persistent Passages but share movement, Combat, building, Heartbeats,
and visual semantics; the project rejects both a symbolic overworld whose
mountains and creatures occupy equivalent strategic tiles and a separate
tactical battle mode. Large geography is represented by many cells and
composed features, while unbounded creature variety comes from authored
Creature Grammar and deterministic instances generated on demand. This keeps
world encounters, settlement construction, and deep delves in one systemic
game, at the cost of requiring Area identity in future addresses and saves.
