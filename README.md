# Untitled Chronicle RPG

A deep persistent-world RPG about discovering power words, combining them into
impossible capabilities, and leaving material consequences in a generated
world that outlives every body the player inhabits. A mature Chronicle grows
from strange discovery into Holdings, autonomous Agents, factional history, and
the world-changing possibility of a Palimpsest.

Goal 2 is complete: Slices 2A, 2B, and 2C passed automated proof and player UAT.
The eight-slot Loadout is real: intrinsic
`Fly` acts on the Incarnation, while `Fly[Stone]` acts on one adjacent durable
loose Stone. Slice 2C implementation and automated proof now pass: the Bell can
end one body, the Chronicle waits without advancing, and a replacement begins
with a fresh Loadout while words and the moved Stone survive. The
engine-independent Chronicle simulation owns every rule and persistent value,
while Godot renders its state and translates player input. Slice 3 has not
begun.
